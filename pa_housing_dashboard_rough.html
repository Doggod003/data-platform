Methodology & data-quality notes: ZHVI is a smoothed, seasonally-adjusted estimate of typical home value (35th–65th percentile), not a transaction average — treat it as an index, not an appraisal. "Implied prior 4-yr %" back-solves what growth must have been in years 2–5 given the 1-yr and 5-yr figures ((1+g5)/(1+g1)−1); a large gap between the 1-yr rate and this baseline is the core consistency test. Rural counties (Elk, Forest, Sullivan, Fulton) have thin transaction volume, so small absolute moves produce large percentage swings — corroborate against county deed records before relying on any single-county figure. Source: Zillow Research public data, pulled via data-platform pipeline.
